Our Mission

At The Gadgeteers, we are dedicated to inspiring young minds through the exciting world of robotics and STEM education. As a community-wide team representing St. Johns County, Florida, we strive to build not just robots, but character, teamwork, and innovation. Through FIRST Robotics Competition, we empower students to become tomorrow's leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics while fostering creativity, problem-solving skills, and gracious professionalism.
